Our Story

Built by pet parents, for pet parents. Because no one should have to choose between their pet's health and their finances.

A mission born from love

Pawsure started when our founder's dog, Max, needed emergency surgery. The unexpected $4,000 vet bill was overwhelming. We realized that traditional pet insurance was complicated, full of hidden fees, and felt too corporate.

We built Pawsure to be different. Warm, transparent, and genuinely caring. We process claims in record time so you can focus on what matters most: helping your best friend recover.

Our Journey

How we grew from a small idea to a nationwide pack of pet lovers.

2021

The Spark

Our founder's dog, Max, needed emergency surgery. The unexpected $4,000 vet bill sparked the idea for a fairer, simpler way to handle pet care costs.

2022

Pawsure is Born

We launched Pawsure with a small team of 5 people and one simple mission: transparent coverage with zero hidden fees and fast, caring claims processing.

2024

50,000+ Pets Covered

We expanded our coverage nationwide and launched our automated claims system, cutting average claim review times down to under 48 hours.

2026

A Smarter Future

Introducing new smart pre-approval features and 1-click vet invoicing directly from the Pawsure Dashboard to make claims even faster.
