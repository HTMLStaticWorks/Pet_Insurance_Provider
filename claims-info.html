Claims Made Simple

Focus on your pet's recovery, not on paperwork. We've designed our claims process to be lightning fast and stress-free.

1

Pay at the Vet

When your pet needs care, visit any licensed veterinarian, specialist, or emergency clinic. You pay the vet directly at the time of service, just like a normal visit. Don't forget to ask for a detailed, itemized invoice!

2

Snap & Upload

Open your Pawsure Dashboard on your phone or computer. Click "Submit a Claim", select your pet, and upload a clear photo or PDF of your itemized vet invoice. No messy claim forms required.

3

Get Paid Fast

Our team reviews most claims within 48 hours. Once approved, we'll reimburse your covered costs via direct deposit straight into your bank account. You can track your claim's status directly in the dashboard.
